Improving NASA Satellite Data
with Low-Cost Sensors

How citizen scientists are helping RTI measure particulate matter in the atmosphere

RTI is working with NASA (as well as the Universities Space Research Association, the South Coast Air Quality Management District, the Environmental Protection Agency, and various other institutions) to deploy low-cost air-quality monitors at ground level, the data from which can be used to provide additional information to help improve satellite readings.
